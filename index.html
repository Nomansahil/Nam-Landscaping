Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle both landscaping and swimming pool maintenance?

Yes, that is exactly what we are built for. One NAM Landscaping team looks after your garden, irrigation and swimming pool under a single contract, so you deal with one supervisor, one invoice and one standard of work.

Which areas of Dubai do you serve?

We serve villas, residential communities and commercial properties across Dubai, including Palm Jumeirah, Emirates Hills, Dubai Hills Estate, Arabian Ranches, Jumeirah Golf Estates, Al Barari, The Meadows, The Springs, JVC, Damac Hills, Tilal Al Ghaf, MBR City and Mirdif. See the full list on our Areas We Serve page.

Is the site visit and quotation really free?

Yes. We visit your property, assess the garden or pool, discuss your goals and send a clear written quotation, free of charge and with no obligation.

How quickly can you start?

Maintenance visits can usually begin within a few days of approval. Design and build projects start once scope and materials are confirmed, your quotation includes a realistic start date and timeline.

Do you offer annual maintenance contracts?

Yes. Our annual maintenance contracts cover scheduled garden and pool visits, water testing, seasonal planting, irrigation checks and priority emergency response, with a written report after every visit.
