Overview

The Right Turf, on the Right Base

Artificial grass succeeds or fails underground. Without a compacted, free-draining base it ripples in heat, puddles in rain and shows every seam within a year. And not all turf survives Dubai: budget fibres fade, flatten and become too hot to walk on. The difference between a lawn that embarrasses and one that fools the neighbours is specification and preparation.

We supply dense, UV-stabilised turf in natural multi-tone greens, install it over a properly graded and compacted sub-base with full drainage, and finish with secure jointing, pinned edges and silica infill. It pairs beautifully with pathways and decking, shaded seating, and a full landscape design when you're ready for the bigger picture.

Artificial Grass vs Natural Lawn

Both have their place in a Dubai garden. Here is an honest side-by-side so you can choose the right surface for each area before you invest.

Good to Know

Artificial Grass: Your Questions

How much does artificial grass cost in Dubai?

As a guide, economy turf starts from about AED 35 per square metre supplied and installed, mid-range from AED 50, premium from AED 75, and dense sports or pet grades from around AED 90 (see the grade table above). The exact rate depends on the turf grade, the ground preparation and drainage needed, and site access, so you get a fixed, itemised quote after a free site visit.

Does artificial grass get too hot in the Dubai sun?

Quality turf gets warm in direct summer sun, that's physics, but lighter multi-tone fibres, heat-reflective yarn technology and a quick rinse before use make a real difference. Shaded placement and pergolas help too; we advise honestly on each area.

How long does artificial grass last here?

Premium UV-stabilised turf, properly installed, gives 8–12 years of good looks in Dubai conditions. The base matters as much as the turf, ours is compacted and drained so the surface stays flat for its whole life.

Is it suitable for pets?

Yes, we install permeable, easy-rinse systems with antibacterial infill options. Solids lift easily, liquids drain through, and an occasional enzyme rinse keeps everything fresh.

Can it be installed over tiles or concrete?

Yes, with the right preparation: drainage falls checked, a shock-pad underlay for comfort, and perimeter fixing instead of pins. Roofs and balconies are a speciality, we use lightweight systems there.

How do I maintain it?

A monthly brush against the pile keeps fibres upright, plus a rinse to clear dust. We can include artificial lawn care in a garden maintenance plan if you'd rather never think about it.

Is artificial grass or a natural lawn better for Dubai?

It depends on the area. Artificial grass wins on water use, summer looks and low maintenance, and it stays green through August with almost no irrigation. A natural lawn suits owners who want living grass and will fund weekly mowing, feeding and heavy watering. Many Dubai gardens use both: real planting and beds, with an artificial lawn in the high-traffic play areas.
