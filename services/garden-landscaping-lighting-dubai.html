Landscape Lighting in Dubai

Landscape Lighting

Good landscape lighting is composition: a palm crown picked out by an uplight, a path defined by soft pools of warm light, a wall texture grazed into relief, the pool glowing as the centrepiece. Bad lighting is one floodlight and a lot of glare. The fittings cost about the same, the design is the difference.

We plan lighting in layers (paths, plants, architecture, water), specify warm IP-rated LED fittings that survive heat and irrigation spray, and wire everything safely with proper outdoor cabling, transformers and smart or timer control. Lighting is best designed alongside your landscape, pergola or water feature, but we retrofit existing gardens cleanly too.

  • Lighting design and night-effect plan
  • Path, step and driveway lighting
  • Tree and plant uplighting
  • Facade and wall grazing
  • Pool, spa and water feature lighting
  • Pergola and seating area lighting
  • Smart control, dimming and scheduling
  • Safe IP-rated wiring and transformers

A second garden, after dark

The space you paid to landscape works evening shifts, dinners, gatherings and quiet late swims, all year.

Safety and security

Lit paths, steps and entrances prevent stumbles and remove the dark corners that make properties feel vulnerable.

Serious kerb appeal

A softly lit facade and front garden photograph beautifully and lift perceived property value instantly.

Efficient to run

Modern warm LEDs sip power; zoned smart control means you light what you're using, when you're using it.

Good to Know

Landscaping & Lighting: Questions, Answered

How much does landscaping cost in Dubai?

Garden landscaping with NAM starts from around AED 120 per square metre, depending on how much is soft planting versus hardscape, the design complexity and the materials. A straightforward refresh can be a few thousand dirhams; full design-and-build projects scale with scope. After a free site visit you get a fixed, itemised quotation.

How long does a villa landscaping project take?

Most villa gardens take three to eight weeks depending on scope. Hardscape and irrigation come first, planting and finishes last. Your quotation includes a realistic week-by-week timeline.

Do I need community or authority approval for landscaping?

Many Dubai communities (Emaar, Nakheel, Damac and others) require NOC approval for external modifications. We prepare the drawings and documents and guide you through your community's process.

Which plants survive Dubai's summer best?

We design with proven performers, bougainvillea, frangipani, date and fan palms, damas alternatives, ornamental grasses and hardy ground covers, placed according to sun exposure and paired with drip irrigation.

Can you maintain the garden after the project?

Yes, and we recommend it. Most clients move onto a garden maintenance plan or a combined garden-and-pool annual contract, so the team that built your landscape is the team that keeps it perfect.

Is outdoor lighting safe with irrigation everywhere?

Yes, when installed correctly: IP65+ fittings, waterproof connectors, proper cable burial depth and RCD protection. This is exactly where DIY installs go wrong, and where our electricians don't.

What colour temperature works best in gardens?

Warm white (2700–3000K) flatters planting, stone and skin tones, it's our default. Cooler light suits some modern architecture and water effects; we mix deliberately, never accidentally.

Can lighting be added to an existing garden without wrecking it?

Yes. We hand-trench around root zones, lift and relay turf cleanly and use existing conduit where possible. The garden looks untouched, except after dark.

Can I control everything from my phone?

If you like, smart transformers and controllers give you zones, dimming, schedules and scenes from an app. Prefer simple? Astronomical timers turn everything on at sunset with zero involvement.

How much does landscape lighting cost?

It scales with garden size and fitting count, a front-entrance scheme is a different project from a full villa garden. The lighting plan we prepare prices everything per zone, so you can phase the installation if you prefer.
