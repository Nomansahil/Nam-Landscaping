Overview

The Built Half of a Great Garden

Hardscape is where corners cut underground surface within a year: pavers laid on poor base sink and rock, cheap composite decking sags in heat, dark stone becomes untouchable by noon. Material choice and groundwork are 80% of the job, and they're invisible in the showroom.

We design and build pathways and driveways, composite and timber decking, outdoor kitchens and BBQ counters, fire pits and seating walls, boundary treatments and raised planters. Every element is chosen for barefoot summer temperatures, sand and UV, and detailed to drain, expand and age gracefully. Hardscape anchors a full landscape design, frames lawns and water features, and pairs naturally with shade structures overhead.

Good to Know

Hardscaping: Your Questions

How much does hardscaping cost in Dubai?

Paving and hardscaping start from AED 75 per square metre supplied and laid for standard finishes. Natural stone, level changes and drainage works raise the figure, so your exact price is fixed in writing after a site visit.

Which paving stays coolest underfoot in Dubai?

Lighter colours and lower-density materials: pale porcelain, light sandstone and travertine stay markedly cooler than dark granite or standard concrete pavers. Around pools we specify from a shortlist we've literally walked barefoot in summer.

Is composite decking better than real wood here?

For most Dubai homes, yes, quality composite resists UV fade, termites and warping with near-zero maintenance. Hardwood looks and feels wonderful but demands annual care. We fit both and explain the honest trade-offs.

Why does paving sink, and how do you prevent it?

Inadequate sub-base, almost every time, paving laid on poorly compacted sand settles unevenly within a year or two. We excavate to proper depth, compact graded base material in layers and detail edge restraints. It's slower and it's why ours stays flat.

Can you build a full outdoor kitchen?

Yes, weatherproofed counters in stone or porcelain, built-in BBQs, sinks, storage and power, with shade and lighting designed overhead. We handle the trades end to end, including drainage and electrical.

Do hardscape changes need community approval?

External works visible from outside the plot, boundary changes, front-yard paving, significant structures, usually need an NOC in Dubai communities. We advise what your project triggers and prepare the paperwork.
