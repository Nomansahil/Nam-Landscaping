Overview

Outdoor Rooms, Engineered for the Gulf

A pergola in Dubai has a harder job than anywhere temperate: intense UV that bleaches cheap finishes, summer surface temperatures that punish bad material choices, and wind loads that demand proper fixing, plus a community NOC before anything goes up. Done right, it adds an entire room to your home for most of the year.

We build powder-coated aluminium pergolas (including motorised louvred roofs that open to the winter sky and seal against summer sun), treated timber structures for a softer look, and tensioned shade sails for pools and play areas. Most projects integrate lighting, fans and planting, and a pergola is the natural anchor of any outdoor living upgrade.

Good to Know

Pergolas & Shade: Your Questions

How much does a pergola cost in Dubai?

A fitted aluminium pergola starts from AED 650 per square metre. A fixed roof costs less than a motorised louvred system, and integrated lighting or fans add to the figure, all confirmed in a written quote after a site visit.

Do I need approval to build a pergola in Dubai?

In nearly all villa communities, yes, developers require an NOC with drawings before installation, and there are height and setback rules. We prepare the documents and manage the approval as part of every project.

Aluminium or wood, which is better for Dubai?

Powder-coated aluminium wins on longevity: no warping, no termites, minimal maintenance under intense UV. Quality treated timber offers warmth and looks beautiful but needs periodic re-finishing. We'll show you both honestly, including what each looks like after five summers.

What is a louvred pergola and is it worth it?

A pergola whose roof blades rotate, open for breeze and stars, closed for full shade or rain. Motorised louvred systems cost more than fixed roofs but transform usability, and they're our most requested structure for dining areas.

How long does installation take?

Once design and NOC are approved, fabrication takes two to four weeks and on-site installation typically two to five days. We confirm the full timeline in your quotation.

Can you add lighting, fans or screens?

Yes, integrated LED lighting, ceiling fans, drop-down screens and privacy slats are all designed in from the start, which always looks better than retrofitting them later.
