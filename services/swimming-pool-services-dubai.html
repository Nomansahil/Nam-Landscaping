Swimming Pool Construction in Dubai

Swimming Pool Construction

Building a pool in Dubai involves more than digging a hole: community NOCs and authority approvals, structural engineering for local soil conditions, waterproofing that must be perfect before finishes go on, and equipment sized for a climate where the pool runs hard ten months a year. Skipping steps here is how pools end up leaking in year three.

We manage the full sequence, design and visualisation, approvals, excavation and structure, waterproofing, premium tile or aggregate finishes, plant room and commissioning. Most clients build the pool as part of a wider landscape project, pairing it with decking, shade and lighting so the whole space is finished together, then keep it perfect on a maintenance plan from day one.

  • Pool design and 3D visualisation
  • Community NOC and authority approvals
  • Excavation and reinforced structure
  • Professional waterproofing systems
  • Tile, mosaic or aggregate finishes
  • Energy-efficient pumps, filtration and salt systems
  • Heating, chilling and automation options
  • Commissioning, handover and first-month care

Decades, not seasons

Correct structure and waterproofing are invisible on day one and priceless in year ten. We build for the long term because we also maintain pools for a living.

Lower running costs

Right-sized variable-speed pumps, efficient filtration and smart automation can halve a pool's energy bill versus builder-grade equipment.

A pool that fits your life

Family splash zone, lap lane, plunge pool for a compact courtyard or a showpiece with a sunken seating edge, designed around how you'll actually use it.

One accountable builder

Structure, finishes, equipment and surrounding landscape from one team, no gaps between contractors for problems to hide in.

Swimming Pool Maintenance in Dubai

Swimming Pool Maintenance

At 40 °C+, pool water changes fast: chlorine burns off in hours, evaporation concentrates salts and calcium, and a missed week can turn a blue pool green. Proper maintenance in Dubai is not occasional cleaning, it is disciplined chemistry plus preventive equipment care, on a schedule that never slips.

Our technicians test and balance your water on every visit, clean thoroughly, and inspect pumps, filters and chlorinators so small faults are fixed before they kill a motor in August. Need a one-off rescue first? See pool cleaning for green-pool recovery, or pool repair if equipment is already down. Garden too? One contract can cover both.

  • Water testing, chlorine, pH, alkalinity, stabiliser
  • Chemical dosing and balancing
  • Skimming, brushing and vacuuming
  • Filter cleaning and backwashing
  • Pump, motor and chlorinator inspection
  • Skimmer and pump baskets emptied
  • Water level and auto-fill check
  • WhatsApp visit report with readings and photos

Your family's health

Correctly sanitised, balanced water prevents algae, bacteria and the eye-and-skin irritation that comes from guesswork dosing.

Your equipment investment

Pumps, heaters and salt cells are expensive. Balanced water and early fault detection routinely double equipment life.

Your pool's finish

Aggressive or scale-forming water destroys tiles, grout and interior finishes, slowly, invisibly, expensively. Balance prevents it.

Your time and peace of mind

The pool is simply always ready. No test kits, no chemical shopping, no Sunday afternoons brushing walls.

Swimming Pool Cleaning in Dubai

Swimming Pool Cleaning

Pool cleaning in Dubai is its own discipline. Fine desert dust passes straight through tired filters, organic debris feeds algae within days, and hard water leaves scale at the waterline. A proper clean treats all three, it's vacuuming and brushing plus filter restoration and water correction, not a quick net-skim.

We handle everything from a single deep clean before a party to full green-pool recovery after months of neglect, plus scheduled cleaning for owners who handle their own chemicals. If testing shows your water or equipment needs ongoing attention, we'll tell you straight and suggest a maintenance plan, and if something is broken, our repair team is the same phone call.

  • Surface skimming and debris removal
  • Wall and floor brushing, tiles, grout, waterline
  • Full pool vacuuming including steps and corners
  • Filter deep clean or backwash
  • Skimmer, basket and weir cleaning
  • Waterline scale and grime treatment
  • Shock treatment and clarification where needed
  • Green pool recovery and re-balancing

Genuinely swim-ready water

Clear isn't the same as clean. We restore sanitiser levels and remove the biofilm and algae spores a surface clean leaves behind.

Filters that actually filter

A clogged filter recirculates dirt. Deep-cleaning the media is the single biggest upgrade most Dubai pools need.

No damage shortcuts

Wrong brushes scratch finishes; careless acid washing ruins grout. Our technicians know what each surface tolerates.

Faster, cheaper than DIY trial-and-error

A green pool fixed wrong goes green again next week. We fix the cause, not the colour.

Swimming Pool Repair in Dubai

Swimming Pool Repair

Dubai is hard on pool equipment: pumps run long hours against heat, salt systems corrode terminals, and UV crazes anything plastic. When something fails, the difference between a good repair company and an expensive one is diagnosis, replacing a capacitor is not the same bill as replacing a pump, and you deserve to know which one you actually need.

We repair pumps and motors, filters, chlorinators, heaters and chillers, LED lighting, valves and pipework, and we trace leaks through pressure testing before recommending anything invasive. Structural work, re-tiling, grout restoration, expansion joint repair, is handled by the same team that builds new pools. After the fix, a maintenance plan keeps the next failure from happening.

  • Pump and motor repair or replacement
  • Filter repairs and media replacement
  • Leak detection and pressure testing
  • Heater and chiller repair
  • Salt chlorinator service and cell replacement
  • Underwater LED light replacement
  • Pipework, valve and fitting repairs
  • Tiling, grouting and finish restoration

Small faults grow teeth

A weeping pump seal becomes a seized motor; a slow leak undermines the deck. Early repairs are routinely a tenth the cost of late ones.

Water loss is money loss

A modest leak can waste thousands of litres a month, you pay for the water, the chemicals dissolved in it, and the damage it does on the way out.

Safety isn't optional

Faulty pool lighting and degraded electrical connections around water are genuinely dangerous. Our electricals work is done properly, to code.

Honest triage saves you money

If a repair isn't worth it, we say so, and quote the replacement option alongside, so you decide with full information.

Good to Know

Swimming Pool Services: Questions, Answered

How long does it take to build a pool in Dubai?

Typically 8–14 weeks on site once approvals are in place, depending on size and finishes. Approvals themselves vary by community, we start that paperwork immediately so it overlaps with design time.

Do I need approval to build a pool at my villa?

Yes, your community developer (and in some cases Dubai Municipality) must approve the design before work starts. We prepare the engineering drawings and manage the application as part of every build.

How much does a swimming pool cost in Dubai?

Villa pools span a wide range depending on size, structure, finishes and equipment. After a site visit we provide a detailed fixed quotation broken down by stage, so you can adjust scope with full visibility.

Concrete or fibreglass, which is better here?

Reinforced concrete remains the standard for Dubai villas: fully custom shapes, premium finishes and proven longevity in local conditions. We'll discuss alternatives honestly where a different approach genuinely suits your site and budget.

Can you renovate an existing pool instead of rebuilding?

Often, yes, new finishes, waterproofing and modern equipment can transform a structurally sound older pool at a fraction of new-build cost. Our assessment tells you honestly which side of the line your pool is on.

How much does pool maintenance cost in Dubai?

Weekly swimming pool maintenance starts from AED 350 per month as a fixed fee, with routine chemicals included. The exact figure depends on pool size and whether garden care is combined, confirmed after a free assessment.

How often does a pool in Dubai need maintenance?

Weekly, as a rule. Heat and dust change water chemistry within days, and a fortnightly gap in summer is how pools turn green. Heavily used or shared pools may need two visits per week in peak season.

What's included in your pool maintenance service?

Every visit: water testing and chemical balancing, skimming, brushing, vacuuming, filter cleaning or backwashing, basket emptying and an equipment inspection, followed by a WhatsApp report with readings and photos.

Are chemicals included in the price?

Yes, routine balancing chemicals are included in our standard plans, so the monthly cost is predictable. Major corrective treatments (like a green-pool rescue at takeover) are quoted separately and agreed first.

Can you maintain the pool while we're away for summer?

Absolutely, it's one of our most common requests. The pool stays balanced and protected while you travel, and the weekly photo report means you can see its condition from anywhere.

Do you service shared and commercial pools?

Yes. We maintain community, gym and hotel pools with the documented testing, dosing records and equipment logs that inspections require.

My pool is green, can it be saved without draining?

Usually yes. Most green pools recover with shock treatment, intensive filtration and brushing over several days. Draining is a last resort we only recommend when debris load or staining genuinely requires it, and we'll show you why before suggesting it.

How long does a deep clean take?

A standard deep clean takes half a day to a day. Green-pool recovery typically needs three to five days, because the filter must clear the water in passes. We give you a timeline with the quote.

How much does pool cleaning cost in Dubai?

One-off cleans depend on pool size and condition, a clear written price follows a quick inspection. Scheduled cleaning visits are priced monthly and cost far less than periodic rescues.

What should I do after a sandstorm?

Run the filter, don't swim, and avoid vacuuming sand into a fragile system, heavy sand can damage pumps. Call us for a storm clean-up: we remove sediment safely and deep-clean the filter afterwards.

Is one-off cleaning available, or only contracts?

Both. Plenty of clients call us once a season or before events. If a contract would genuinely save you money, we'll say so, but there's never pressure.

How do I know if my pool has a leak?

The bucket test is a good first check: a bucket of water on a step should evaporate at the same rate as the pool. If the pool drops faster, suspect a leak. We confirm with pressure testing of lines and a methodical inspection before quoting any work.

How fast can you attend an emergency repair?

Usually same or next working day across Dubai, with priority response for maintenance-contract clients. Equipment failures in summer are treated as urgent, water quality degrades within days of a dead pump.

Should I repair or replace an old pump?

Rule of thumb: if the repair costs more than half a new pump, or the pump is past 8–10 years, replacement usually wins, modern variable-speed pumps also cut running costs noticeably. We quote both options so the choice is yours.

Do you guarantee repair work?

Yes. Workmanship is guaranteed, and parts carry their manufacturer warranties. If something we fixed fails within the guarantee period, we return and make it right at no cost.

Can you repair pools you didn't build or maintain?

Absolutely, most of our repair work is on pools we're seeing for the first time. We diagnose from scratch and document everything we find.
