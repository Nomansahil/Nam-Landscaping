Water Features in Dubai

Water Features

Most failed water features fail predictably: evaporation outpaces top-up, hard-water scale chokes pumps, algae blooms in warm still corners, and the 'relaxing' feature becomes a weekly chore. Every one of those problems is solvable at the design stage, auto-fill systems, accessible pump chambers, correct flow rates and finishes that hide mineral marks.

We build wall cascades and spillways, freestanding and sculptural fountains, reflecting pools, pondless waterfalls (all the sound, none of the open water) and feature walls that double as privacy screens. Paired with underwater lighting they become the garden's evening centrepiece, and as part of a full landscape design or pool build, they tie the whole space together.

  • Reflecting pools and rills
  • Pondless waterfalls and rock features
  • Auto-fill and water treatment systems

Irrigation Installation & Repair in Dubai

Irrigation Installation & Repair

Most struggling Dubai gardens share one root cause: irrigation that was never designed, only installed. Identical emitters on palms and ground cover, zones that flood one bed and starve the next, controllers still on winter timing in July. The plants get the blame; the pipework deserves it.

We design zoned systems matched to planting, drip for beds and trees, pop-ups for lawns, with smart controllers that adjust seasonally and stop watering when it rains. Existing system playing up? We trace leaks, replace clogged emitters, rebalance zones and reprogram schedules. Irrigation is also where every landscaping project of ours begins, and checking it is built into every maintenance visit.

  • Irrigation design zoned to planting type
  • Drip line and emitter installation
  • Pop-up sprinkler systems for lawns
  • Smart Wi-Fi controllers and timers
  • Leak detection and pipe repair
  • Emitter cleaning and replacement
  • Pump and tank connection work
  • Seasonal schedule programming

Good to Know

Water Features & Irrigation: Questions, Answered

Aren't water features wasteful in a desert city?

Designed responsibly, no. Recirculating features reuse the same water continuously; losses come only from evaporation and splash, which good design minimises. A typical feature uses a small fraction of what a natural lawn of the same footprint would drink.

How much maintenance does a water feature need?

With auto-fill and proper treatment built in: topping up chemicals occasionally, a seasonal pump-chamber clean, and a yearly service. Features we design are deliberately boring to own, that's the point. We can also fold care into a maintenance contract.

Will it attract mosquitoes?

Moving water doesn't, mosquitoes need still water to breed. Circulation, skimming and correct treatment keep features mosquito-free; stagnant neglected ponds are the culprit, and we don't build those.

Can a feature work in a small courtyard or entrance?

Small spaces are where water works hardest, a slim wall cascade or ceramic fountain transforms a courtyard. Scale and proportion matter more than size; that's a design job, and it's ours.

What about Dubai's hard water and scale?

We specify scale-tolerant finishes, dose against mineral build-up and design pump access for easy descaling. White crusty fountains are a specification failure, avoidable from day one.

Drip irrigation or sprinklers, which do I need?

Usually both, zoned separately: drip lines for planting beds, trees and hedges (precise, low-evaporation watering at the roots) and pop-up sprinklers for lawn areas. A mixed, properly zoned system is the Dubai standard we install.

How often should garden irrigation run in Dubai?

It changes through the year, typically early-morning daily watering in summer, reduced frequency in winter. Smart controllers handle this automatically; it's also exactly what we tune on every maintenance visit.

Why are my drippers always blocking?

Dubai's hard water leaves calcium deposits inside emitters, and fine sand finds its way into lines. Filters at the source, periodic flushing and pressure-compensating emitters largely solve it, we build all three into our installs.

Can you connect irrigation to a water tank and pump?

Yes, including pump selection and pressure setup, tank float protection and filtration. Many villas run irrigation from a dedicated tank, and we configure the whole chain.

How do I know if my system is leaking underground?

Unexplained water bills, persistently damp paving, unusually lush patches or pressure drop across a zone are the classic signs. We pressure-test zone by zone to locate the fault before any digging.
